# 📚 Curriculum Deep-Dive — Phase by Phase

This document provides a granular breakdown of all 79 items in Nia's Language Adventure. Each item is tied to a specific pedagogical goal.

---

## 🛠️ Phase 1: The Foundation (Devanagari Mastery)
**Goal**: Transition from "looking at shapes" to "knowing sounds".

### Sanskrit Track
1.  **S1-00D**: *Why Sanskrit?* — Motivation and the "Secret Superpower" angle.
2.  **S1-01**: *The Devanagari Story* — The history of the script used for both languages.
3.  **S1-02 to S1-04A**: *Vowels Part 1 & 2* — Mastery of Swar (अ, आ, इ, ई...).
4.  **S1-05A to S1-05D**: *Consonants Deep-Dive* — Pronunciation guide for every letter in the Devanagari alphabet.
5.  **S1-06A to S1-06B**: *First Words & Reading Power* — Combining letters to form basic sounds.
6.  **S1-GUIDE**: Complete reference with audio-visual letter charts.
7.  **S1-QUIZ**: 30 questions focusing on letter identification and sound matching.

### Marathi Track
1.  **M1-01 to M1-05**: *Marathi Alphabets* — Focusing on the slight variations in script style and sounds.
2.  **M1-GUIDE**: The "ळ" letter special focus and Marathi matra tables.
3.  **M1-QUIZ**: 30 questions on Marathi-specific phonetics.

### Bridge Track
1.  **B1-01 to B1-02**: *The Script Connect* — Direct comparisons showing Nia that if she knows one, she knows 90% of the other.

---

## 🍎 Phase 2: Building Blocks (Vocabulary)
**Goal**: Build a functional vocabulary of 100+ words across 7 different themes.

### Sanskrit Track
1.  **S2-01 to S2-07**: Thematic exploration.
    - *Body Parts*: Hand, eye, head in Sanskrit.
    - *Nature*: Sun, moon, trees, rivers.
    - *Animals*: Forest and farm animal names.
    - *Numbers*: Sanskrit numbers from 1 to 20.
    - *Food & Family*: Essential daily vocabulary.
2.  **S2-GUIDE**: 7 vocabulary themes with 100+ word lookup tables.
3.  **S2-QUIZ**: Memory retention challenge covering all 7 themes.

### Marathi Track
1.  **M2-01 to M2-07**: Daily life vocabulary.
    - *Home & School*: Objects Nia interacts with daily.
    - *Festivals*: Cultural vocabulary for Diwali, Holi, etc.
    - *Food*: Traditional Marathi dishes and ingredients.
2.  **M2-GUIDE**: Comprehensive Marathi vocabulary lookup.
3.  **M2-QUIZ**: 30 questions on daily usage words.

---

## 🧩 Phase 3: The Engine (Grammar & Sentences)
**Goal**: Move from single words to structured logic.

### Sanskrit Track
1.  **S3-01 to S3-03**: *Sentence Construction* — Introducing the logic of Cases (Vibhakti) and Verbs (Lakar).
2.  **S3-04 to S3-05**: *Shlokas Decoded* — Applying grammar to understand ancient wisdom verses.
3.  **S3-06**: *Sandhi Rules* — How sounds merge when you speak fast.
4.  **S3-GUIDE**: Sentence building blocks and verb conjugation tables.
5.  **S3-QUIZ**: Grammar logic and sentence arrangement challenges.

### Marathi Track
1.  **M3-01 to M3-05**: *Conversational Competence* — Learning how to ask questions and express feelings.
2.  **M3-06**: *A Day in Nia's Life* — Applying full sentences to a daily routine.
3.  **M3-GUIDE**: Tense charts and common conversational templates.
4.  **M3-QUIZ**: Dialogue completion and tense identification.

---

## 🕊️ Phase 4: The Spirit (Stories & Culture)
**Goal**: Reading actual literature and understanding historical context.

### Sanskrit Track
1.  **S4-01 to S4-03**: *Panchatantra* — Analyzing the logic and moral of classical fables.
2.  **S4-04**: *Subhashitas* — Wisdom in two lines.
3.  **S4-05**: *Language of Epics* — Introduction to the Ramayana and Mahabharata.
4.  **S4-GUIDE**: Full texts of stories with Sanskrit-to-English translations.
5.  **S4-QUIZ**: Reading comprehension and ethical logic.

### Marathi Track
1.  **M4-01 to M4-02**: *Marathi Lore* — Folk tales and the wisdom of the Sant-Kavis (Saint-Poets).
2.  **M4-03**: *Music & Art* — Introduction to Lavani, Ovi, and traditional songs.
3.  **M4-04**: *Identity* — How Shivaji Maharaj protected and refined the Marathi language.
4.  **M4-05**: *Nia Reads Aloud* — Graduation milestone.
5.  **M4-GUIDE**: Cultural heritage maps and literature excerpts.
6.  **M4-QUIZ**: Cultural history and folklore comprehension.

---

**Appendix**: The "Bridge" modules (B1-B4) serve as the glue for each phase, ensuring Nia never feels like she is learning two unrelated subjects.
