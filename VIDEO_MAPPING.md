## Instructions

1. **Upload videos to YouTube** and organize them in your playlist
2. **Copy the YouTube link** for each video (e.g., `https://youtu.be/zugOABVMq1A`)
3. **Fill in this file** with the corresponding video code
4. **Send this completed file** to your developer to update the app
5. Each video will then be playable directly in the app!

### Example
```
S1-01 : https://youtu.be/zugOABVMq1A
S1-02 : https://youtu.be/dQw4w9WgXcQ
M1-01 : https://youtu.be/jNQXAC9IVRw
```

**Note:** You can also paste links directly in the app by clicking on any video card and using the YouTube link input field!
